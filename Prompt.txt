-'Terms and Conditions'
-'Privacy Policy'

Both should be plain text links, no styling needed.
Both the links should appear on its own separate line.

Create a Terms and Conditions page for the expense tracker app.

1. Register a GET /terms route that renders templates/terms.html.
2. Create templates/terms.html that extends @templates/base.html.
3. The page should contain generic Terms and Conditions content appropriate for a personal expense tracking app.
   Include the following sections:
   - Acceptance of Terms
   - Use of Service
   - User Accounts and Responsibilities
   - Data and Privacy
   - Limitation of Liability
   - Changes to Terms
   - Contact Information
