Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you register an account, such as your name and email address. We also collect the expense data you enter into the app, including amounts, categories, and dates.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except where required by law or necessary to operate the service (e.g. hosting providers). Any third-party service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools that collect personal data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect app functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also request a copy of your data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
